> 1890 (Weisstein, Ringel 1968, 1974)


Let G be embeddable to a surface S with [[Euler's characteristic]] $2−2γ$, $γ >0$. Then

Moreover, a complete graph $K_f(γ)$ ([[complete graph]]) is embeddable on S, unless S is a [[Klein bottle]]. For a Klein bottle $f(γ) = 7$, however, $χ(G) ≤6$ and $K_6$ is embeddable on the Klein bottle.
