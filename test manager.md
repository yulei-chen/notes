#testing 



Tasks of the test manager:
- Initiate analysis and design, implementation and execution and control
- **Update** [[test plan|planning]] based on test results and progress
- Initiate necessary (counter-)measures
- **[[test schedule|Schedule]]** testing
- Create test reports using the available information
- Selection or definition of metrics 
	- to measure testing process
	- to rate the quality of both testing and product
- **[[Tool selection]]** and planning of necessary trainings
- Determine the **[[degree of test automation]]**
