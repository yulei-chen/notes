#theorems #graph-theory 

Let $\chi$ be a set of [[k-connected|3-connected]] graphs, G be edge-maximal with respect to not containing [[TX]] for any $X \in \chi$. Let S be a [[cut vertex|vertex cut]] of G, $|S|≤2$, $|S|= κ(G)$ ([[connectivity]]). Then $G= G_1 ∪ G_2$, $V(G_1) ∩ V(G_2) = S$, $G_i$ is edge-maximal without TX for any $X ∈ \chi$ and S [[induced|induces]] an edge.

> S induces an edge: 如果 S 里有两个顶点，那么**这两个顶点之间在图 G 中一定有一条边**。
